Giorgia Mollo

Giorgia Mollo is a well-known talented singer-songwriter with a unique artistic flair. Growing up in Calabria, located in the south of Italy and moving to the Uk in 2012, she gained a Music Business degree. With a qualification in singing and songwriting, Giorgia has perfected and shaped her music ide captivating, powerful and warm artist. Opening up shows and performing for established artists such as Alvaro Soler, Vinicio Capossela, Luca Barbarossa, Patty Pravo, Mariella Nava, also with her original music being showcased on RAI 1 for the tv series 'Basta un paio di Baffi', directed by Fabrizio Costa in January 2019. Giorgia has entrepreneurial qualities which have led her to become the founder of People Love To Dance festival, specialising in live and electronic music, this event takes place annually in Calabria also known as the California of Europe.
