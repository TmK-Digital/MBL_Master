Our Mission

Music Beats Life is an independent record label, as well as a production entertainment business, specialising in multiple genres within the digital and acoustic music world. Its base roots are in the UK and Italy. Our slogan describes the connection between music and life, representing the impact that different music frequencies can have on our developments and growth.


A piece of music doesn't need a beat to exist, but when a melody meets its beat, they become one and create a new life, in which melody and beat become part of the same identity. ‘Music Beats Life’ aims to become more than a slogan, as it represents the power through which music allows us to get through experiences, difficulties and challenges in our lives.
